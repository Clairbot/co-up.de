Save co.up!

We’ve been informed that the rent for co.up will be more than doubling in 2020 – and we need your help.

{% include progress-tracker.html %}

In order for us to continue offering our space to the community, we need more people to help us financially offset the cost of running co.up. Our goal has always been to help support better representation in tech, and that means that we always offer our space as affordably as possible. Now we need your support in order to ensure that others will be able to access these same resources going into the future.

Here’s how you can help:

Become a supporting member

Make a monthly contribution that goes directly into supporting the space and supporting the groups that use it.

10€ / month 20€ / month 50€ / month 100€ / month 200€ / month

If you would like to donate a different recurring amount, please reach out to us! We are very open to a custom monthly donation and can set that up for you.

Make a donation

Not ready to become a supporting member yet? Every little bit helps us reach our goal of continuing to offer co.up affordably to community organizations.

Donate on Ko-Fi Donate via Paypal

Share your co.up story with us

Has an event at co.up changed your life? Helped your career? Made you laugh? We want to hear from you! Submit your co.up story »

Watch our #saveCoUp info evening stream
