Support co.up as a meetup organizer

You probably know that we use the community space at co.up for our events, and we’ve got some important news about the space. Their rent will be doubling in 2020 and without immediate financial help they’ll be forced to close their doors. It won’t just affect us: co.up provides free event space for dozens of community organizations and without a major shift in support they will have to close their doors for good in January 2020. We don’t want this to happen!

Here are a few ways you can help #saveCoUp:

  • Inform your community and donate: Let your attendees know what they space is that they are using, and that it needs help to survive. Let them know how they can donate: In the tip jar in the space, via Paypal, via Ko-Fi, or ideally: as a monthly membership supporter! Recurring support is vital for our ability to keep co.up open.
    Become a support member Donate via Paypal Donate on Ko-Fi
  • Spread the word on your social media channels: Help co.up by raising awareness for the situation. This is the key web page you can link to: co-up.de/support-us. Everything you can do to share this with your networks will improve our chances of getting enough support. Nothing is as powerful as your personal stories, so if you are comfortable, please tell the world why co.up matters to you!
  • Find meetup sponsors to help co.up: Some meetups have found creative ways to allow sponsors at thir meetups, and direct the income to co.up, while ensuring that the qualityf and independece of the meetup is not impacted. For example, upfront.ug offers 1 minute announcement slots for a fixed price. This sponsor offer template is free for every meetup to copy and adapt!
  • Talk to your company about becoming a company supporter: If your company might be interested in becoming a supporter of co.up, talk to them about the benefits co.up can offer. For a rundown of what it means to be a co.up company supporter check out our sponsorship offers. We also collected some common arguments and counters for your conversation with your boss or marketing department.

Still have questions? Our FAQ goes over further details about the space, the campaign, and other things you may not know about co.up. And don’t hesitate to reach out to co.up with questions at hello@co-up.de.
